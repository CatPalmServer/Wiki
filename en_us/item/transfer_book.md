### Saved Waypoints List
You can save up to 36 waypoint coordinates. To add a waypoint:

<table>
    <tr><td>Have renamed<br/><a href="transfer.md">Ender Pearl and Emerald</a> ready</td><td><img src="https://i.imgur.com/Gi21eXy.png" width="720"/></td></tr>
    <tr><td>Press<code>Shift+Right-click</code> on the destination block.</td><td><img src="https://i.imgur.com/S9jUyYi.png" width="720"/><br/>Once placed, waypoints cannot be retrieved.</td></tr>
</table>

Waypoints will be sorted automatically by name. [Ender Pearls](transfer.md) can have a maximum of 64 identical entries.
If you don't have enough levels to use [Emeralds](transfer.md), they will disappear instead of damaging you.
